About ZenPack

Welcome, I'm the creator of ZenPack, something I came up with because of my need to eat healthy without spending a ton of time or money. While searching for convenient meal options, I discovered companies like Icon Meals, Factor, Blue Apron, and others that offer great products but at sky-high prices especially if you wanted them to replace all your meals for the day. I decided to see if I could do it on my own and that led me to making multiple excel sheets, meal plans, and eventually this website to share with other people. My Mission

My mission with ZenPack is to share what I created and help you simplify your life, save money, and still eat a balanced diet. The meal plans are filled with premade meals you can just heat up, making it super easy to hit your nutritional goals. This way, you get the same convenience as those expensive services but without breaking the bank.
